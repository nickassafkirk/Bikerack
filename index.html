Car Racks

At the Bikerack we are Ireland's experts in car bicycle carrier racks. We stock a large range of racks to suit most models of cars from Thule, Buzzrack and Hollywood. We also offer free domestic shipping on all bicycle racks and can assist with finding and fitting the correct rack for your vehicle.

Boot Mount Racks

Boot mounted racks fit most models of car and are great for saloon, estate and hatchback vehicles.

Boot mounted racks are affordable, versatile and fit a broad range of cars. They can typically be folded away and stored in the boot when not in use.

Towball Mount Racks

Towball mounted racks provide quick and secure mounting to cars with a towball fitted.

Towball mounted racks mount lower to the ground and are great for heavier bikes or carrying more than 3 bikes.

Roof Racks

Roof mounted bike racks are great way to carry bikes when easy access to the boot is required.

Roof racks can be combined with towball or boot mounted racks if more than 5 bikes need to be transported.

Roof racks require roof bars which are sold sepparately and are typically not suitable for shorter users.
